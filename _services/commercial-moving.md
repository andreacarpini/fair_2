## Commercial Moving Tips

### Planning Phase
- Start planning 2-3 months in advance
- Create a detailed inventory
- Communicate early with employees
- Coordinate with building management
- Plan for technology setup

### Preparation Phase
- Label everything clearly
- Back up important data
- Notify customers and vendors
- Arrange utility transfers
- Prepare new space

- Have a point person available
- Keep important items with you
- Monitor progress closely
- Address issues immediately
- Document any concerns

### Post-Move Phase
- Test all systems
- Update address information
- Notify stakeholders
- Conduct employee orientation
- Evaluate the move process
