About Chicago Pro Movers

Your trusted moving partner in the Windy City. We've been helping Chicagoans move with confidence and peace of mind since 2010.

Our Story

Chicago Pro Movers was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide Chicago residents and businesses with reliable, professional moving services at fair prices. What started as a small operation with just one truck and two movers has grown into one of the city's most trusted moving companies.

Our founders, lifelong Chicagoans themselves, understood the unique challenges of moving in the Windy City. From navigating narrow staircases in walk-up apartments to dealing with Chicago's unpredictable weather, we've faced and overcome every moving challenge imaginable.

Today, we operate a fleet of modern moving trucks and employ a team of over 50 professional movers, all trained in our proven techniques for safe, efficient moving. But despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our core values: reliability, transparency, and exceptional customer service.
