Our Story

Chicago Pro Movers was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide Chicago residents and businesses with reliable, professional moving services at fair prices. What started as a small operation with just two trucks and a handful of dedicated movers has grown into one of the city's most trusted moving companies.

Over the past 14 years, we've helped over 15,000 families and businesses relocate across Chicago and beyond. We've moved people from studio apartments to mansions, helped startups expand to new offices, and assisted families in starting new chapters in their lives.

What sets us apart is our team. Every mover is background-checked, professionally trained, and genuinely cares about making your move as smooth as possible. We understand that moving can be stressful, which is why we go above and beyond to provide exceptional service from start to finish.

Today, we're proud to be Chicago's go-to moving company, known for our reliability, professionalism, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you're moving across the street or across the country, we're here to make your move stress-free.
