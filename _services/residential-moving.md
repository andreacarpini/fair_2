# Residential Moving Services in Chicago

Moving to a new home should be an exciting experience, not a stressful one. At Chicago Pro Movers, we specialize in residential moves of all sizes, from studio apartments to large family homes. Our professional team handles every aspect of your move with care and precision, ensuring your belongings arrive safely at your new home.

## Our Residential Moving Process

### 1. Free Consultation and Quote
We start with a detailed consultation to understand your specific moving needs. Our moving specialists will assess the size of your move, discuss any special requirements, and provide you with a transparent, binding quote.

### 2. Pre-Move Planning
Once you book with us, we assign a dedicated moving coordinator who will work with you to plan every detail of your move. We'll help you choose the right moving date, discuss packing options, and answer any questions you may have.

### 3. Professional Packing (Optional)
If you choose our packing service, our trained professionals will carefully pack all your belongings using high-quality materials. We label everything clearly and create a detailed inventory to ensure nothing gets lost.

### 4. Moving Day
On moving day, our professional team arrives on time with all the necessary equipment. We protect your furniture with moving blankets, disassemble items as needed, and load everything securely onto our clean, modern trucks.

### 5. Delivery and Setup
At your new home, we unload everything carefully, reassemble furniture, and place items in the rooms you specify. We won't leave until you're completely satisfied with the placement of your belongings.

## Preparing for Your Residential Move

### One Month Before
- Book your moving company
- Start decluttering and donating items
- Begin collecting moving supplies if self-packing
- Notify important contacts of your address change

### Two Weeks Before
- Confirm moving details with your coordinator
- Pack non-essential items if self-packing
- Arrange for utility transfers
- Plan for children and pets on moving day

### One Week Before
- Complete most packing if self-packing
- Confirm elevator and parking arrangements
- Prepare appliances for moving
- Create a "first night" box with essentials

### Moving Day
- Be ready when the movers arrive
- Do a final walkthrough before leaving
- Keep important documents with you
- Have payment ready for the movers
