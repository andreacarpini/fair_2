Our Story

Built on trust, professionalism, and a commitment to making moving stress-free

Fairplay Moving was founded in 2014 with a simple mission: to provide Chicago residents and businesses with moving services they could trust. After witnessing too many friends and family members struggle with unreliable movers, hidden fees, and damaged belongings, we knew there had to be a better way.

Starting with just one truck and a small team of dedicated professionals, we've grown to become one of Chicago's most trusted moving companies. Our growth has been built entirely on referrals from satisfied customers who appreciate our transparent pricing, professional service, and commitment to treating every move as if it were our own.

Today, we handle over 2,000 moves per year, from studio apartments to large corporate relocations. Despite our growth, we remain committed to the same principles that guided us from day one: honesty, reliability, and fair play in everything we do.
