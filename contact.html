Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book my move?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving season (May-September). However, we can often accommodate last-minute moves with 48-72 hours notice.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes! We provide high-quality boxes, packing tape, bubble wrap, and other materials. We can also handle all your packing if you prefer a full-service move.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Chicago and most surrounding suburbs, including Oak Park, Evanston, Cicero, Berwyn, and many others. Contact us if you're unsure about your area.

Are my belongings insured?

Yes, we provide basic coverage at no additional cost. We also offer additional insurance options for valuable items. All our moves are fully licensed and insured.

Can you move specialty items like pianos?

Absolutely! Our team is trained to handle pianos, artwork, antiques, and other specialty items. Let us know about these items when you book your move.
